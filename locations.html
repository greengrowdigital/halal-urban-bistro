Hillside Flagship

Easy to find. Easier to smell.

The flagship sits between 235th and 236th on Hillside Ave, across from the corner deli, two blocks east of the LIRR station. Look for the red awning and the line.

Park on Hillside (two-hour metered, free after 7pm) or take a Lyft — the patio is the easy meeting point.

Getting Here

From anywhere in NY.

F

By subway

Take the F train to 169th St / Hillside Ave, then the Q43 bus eastbound 8 stops to 233rd St. About 35 minutes from Midtown.

Q43

By bus

The Q43, Q83, and Q1 all stop within a block. Q43 is the fastest from western Queens — 25 minutes from Jamaica Center.

CAR

By car

Take Grand Central Pkwy exit 23 to Cross Island Pkwy south to Hillside Ave exit. Street parking on Hillside is metered until 7pm — free after.
