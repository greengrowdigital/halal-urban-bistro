Our Story

A burger spot built on Dad's recipes.

The Halal Urban Bistro started with Dad's spice blend — the one he used to make scrambled eggs on weekends. We put it on a smashburger, then on a croissant, then on a paratha. Somewhere between the food truck and the brick-and-mortar, a neighborhood found its spot.

Every patty is hand-formed. Every order is made when you walk up. We're closed Mondays because rest matters, and we'll never be the cheapest — only the most honest.
